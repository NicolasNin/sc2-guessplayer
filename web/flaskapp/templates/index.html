What is SC2guess ?

Sc2guess is a project which aims at finding the players in a starcraft 2 replay. It uses databases of replays such as the WCS where the names in the replay are usually correct.

How does it works ?

It assumes that a player (at least a good player), have regular patterns in keyboard use. In particular it looks at the hotkeys a player uses and compares it to the one in the database
